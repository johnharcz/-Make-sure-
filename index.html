Game mode

Select a mode:

Game

Word bank

Arrow keys move between words. Enter or Space selects the focused word. Tab moves to answer slots. Delete or Backspace on a filled slot returns it to the bank.

Your answer slots

Slots fill left to right. Next slot has a thick blue border. Shift+Tab returns to word bank. Tab moves to buttons.

Type the correct word letter by letter. Wrong letters are rejected immediately and buzz. Correct words lock green and the cursor advances. After each correct word JAWS reads the verse so far.

Learn the verse in short phrase chunks. Press Hear phrase, then type the phrase. Wrong letters are rejected immediately. When the phrase is correct, the app moves to the next phrase automatically.

Verse so far

Several words in the verse are hidden. Type each missing word in the blank. Wrong letters are rejected and buzz. Correct words lock green and the cursor advances.

For each position in the verse, four word choices are shown. Press 1, 2, 3, or 4 on the keyboard to pick that choice, or press the first letter of a word to jump focus to it. Tab to a button and press Enter or Space also works. Correct answers lock in place; wrong answers buzz.

Memorized verses

Verses appear here automatically when you complete a game correctly. You can play them back one at a time using the practice buttons next to each verse, or press Play all verses to hear them read aloud in sequence. Press Remove to take a verse off the list when you no longer need it here.
